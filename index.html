Our Story

The Story

Mo & Mo restaurant is a globally influenced Thai-inspired multi- cuisine restaurant nestled in the heart of Chiangmai. Our chefs bring with them years of experience, and a taste for innovation grounded in authenticity. Mo & Mo offers a diverse array of gastronomic adventures that cater to every palette. Our signature Royal Thai cuisine sets the standard for dining excellence, and is prepared by expertly trained chefs endowed with great patience and attention to detail. The result is a deliciously diverse, colorful and delicate menu.

Sourcing fresh ingredients both locally and internationally, guests dining with us get to feast on a delectably diverse range of healthy and appetizing fare.

Mo & Mo also distinguishes itself by emphasizing on the social component of dining out, and taking a vibe-driven approach to fine dining.

Whether you are joining us for dinner, cocktails, a private party or any other special occasion, Mo & Mo welcomes you and promises to make the experience special and memorable.

Come dine with us, and experience, quite literally –
Mo’ & Mo’ than you ever imagined…
